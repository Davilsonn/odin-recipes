Caldo Verde

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Bacon
  • Kale
  • Pepperoni Sausage
  • Water
  • Chicken Broath

Steps

  1. Boil the potatoes until they're mushy. Boil them in the water, broath, salt and oil
  2. Blend the potatoes on a food processor or blender
  3. Pan fry the bacon and sausage. Remember the bacon takes longer to fry
  4. Add the potate blend into the pan and mix
  5. Add the kale and mix until the mixture turns green
  6. ENJOY!
