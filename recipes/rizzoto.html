Rizzoto

Estimated time: 1 hour and 30 minutes

Difficulty: Medium

Good luck!

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Arborio Rice
  • Lemons
  • White Wine
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Chicken/Vegetable Broth
  • Onions

Steps

  1. Prepare the broath aheat of time
  2. Cook the diced onions on a pan until browned
  3. Add the arborio rice
  4. Add the white wine
  5. Mix until dry
  6. Add the broath slowly, only 1 ladle before mixing until dry, than add another one. Continue until texture is pleasant
  7. Add whatever ingredients you want, examples include shrimp, lemon, mushrooms, and chicken
  8. Add butter and the shreded parmesan. Mix until the rizzoto acquires a shimmer
  9. ENJOY!
